About SettlePad

SettlePad keeps track of your IUOs and UOmes. You can send them via email or phone number.

Our algorithm minimizes your receivables by canceling them out against your payables where possible.

Your receivables are allocated as much as possible to your favorite connections. So you can pay beer for A (who is a lousy debtor) and get it back from B.

You can access SettlePad via the web, the iOS app or through our open API.
